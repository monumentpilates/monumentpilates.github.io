Absolutely! Our Fundamentals class is specifically designed for beginners with no prior Pilates experience. Everyone starts somewhere — we make sure you feel comfortable and confident from your very first class.

You can book classes directly through our online booking system powered by WellnessLiving. Simply create an account, browse the schedule, and reserve your spot. We recommend booking in advance as classes fill quickly!

If you're new to Pilates or returning after a break, start with Fundamentals. This class builds your foundation and teaches you proper form on all apparatus. After completing 5 Fundamentals classes, you can progress to Mixed Level. If you have prior Pilates experience and want to skip Fundamentals, email us at howdy@monumentpilates.com for an Experience Check.

Wear comfortable, form-fitting clothing that allows your instructor to see your alignment and lets you move freely. Avoid loose or baggy clothing that can get caught in the equipment. Grip socks are recommended to prevent slipping. We also suggest removing jewellery and tying back long hair.

Pilates is widely recommended by physical therapists and doctors for rehabilitation and pain management. However, please let your instructor know about any injuries or conditions before class so they can offer appropriate modifications. For specific concerns, a Private session is an excellent way to get customized guidance. Always consult your healthcare provider if you're unsure.

Please consult with your healthcare provider first. Many people safely practice Pilates during pregnancy with appropriate modifications. We recommend private sessions during pregnancy so your instructor can tailor every exercise to your needs and trimester. Let us know during booking so we can prepare accordingly.
