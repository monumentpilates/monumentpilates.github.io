Our Story

From Hospitality to Pilates

Monument Pilates is founded by a wife and husband team who spent decades in the restaurant and bar industry. That hospitality DNA flows through everything we do — you'll always feel welcomed, comfortable, and cared for.

Joseph Pilates said, "Physical fitness is the first requisite of happiness." We pay homage to that 100+ year history while recognizing that some great adaptations have occurred along the way. That duality — the old and the new — is what our program is built around.
