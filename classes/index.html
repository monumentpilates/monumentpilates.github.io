Beginner Friendly

Fundamentals Group Class

A supportive and informative introduction to Pilates designed to teach you fundamental skills and principles — with emphasis on familiarizing yourself with the Mat, Reformer, Tower, and Wunda Chair.

You'll learn essential foundational movements, develop body awareness, understand proper alignment, and learn how to safely set up each piece of equipment. No prior experience required — just a willingness to learn!

  • ✓ Perfect for beginners or those returning after a break
  • ✓ Learn all four apparatus types
  • ✓ Hands-on form corrections
  • ✓ Build confidence to progress

Our Equipment

Premium Balanced Body Apparatus

All USA-made equipment chosen to honour the original while adapting for modern bodies.

🔄

Centerline Reformer

Smooth-gliding carriages with leather straps and handles. The foundation of most classes — develops core strength, flexibility, and control.

🗼

Cadillac / Tower

Versatile apparatus for spring-assisted exercises that challenge balance, coordination, and full-body integration.

💺

Wunda Chair

Compact but mighty — challenges stability and strength with spring-loaded pedal exercises for legs, arms, and core.

🧘

Mat Work

The foundation of Pilates. Bodyweight exercises that develop deep core engagement, spinal articulation, and mind-body connection.

Benefits

Why Pilates Is for Everyone

Whether you're an athlete, desk worker, or recovering from injury — Pilates transforms how you move and feel.

💪

Core Strength

Build a strong powerhouse that supports your spine and improves every movement in daily life.

🧠

Mind-Body Connection

Develop awareness of how your body moves. Improve concentration, reduce stress, and find your flow.

🦴

Posture & Alignment

Counteract the effects of sitting and screen time. Stand taller, move more efficiently, reduce pain.

🔧

Injury Recovery

Low-impact with modifiable resistance. Many physical therapists recommend Pilates for rehabilitation.

🏃

Athletic Performance

Cross-train with Pilates to improve flexibility, balance, and core stability for any sport.

😊

Happiness

Joseph Pilates said it: "Physical fitness is the first requisite of happiness." You'll leave every class feeling better than when you walked in.
