About Monument Pilates

Awesome Certified Teachers · Amazing Equipment · Approachable Classes

Our Story

From Hospitality to Movement

Monument Pilates is a family-owned studio founded by a wife and husband team who spent decades working in bars and restaurants. (If you ever want to talk about great places to eat and drink, chat us up!)

When dreaming up Monument, we intentionally skipped both the loud and dark or bougie and snooty experience to create a warm, lived-in space where you feel immediately welcomed and like a regular.

Joseph Pilates said, "Physical fitness is the first requisite of happiness." We pay homage to that history while recognizing that some great adaptations have occurred along the way. That duality — the old and the new, the original and the evolved — is what our program is built around.

Our Approach

Classical vs. Contemporary

We honour both traditions and let you experience the profound benefits of each.

Classical Pilates

A comprehensive immersion into Joseph Pilates' original work. The Mat and Reformer flow in their classical order, supplemented by Tower and Wunda Chair exercises. Precision, control, and flow — engaging deep core muscles while lengthening and strengthening the entire body.

Modifications accommodate all levels, ensuring everyone experiences this time-tested discipline.

Contemporary Pilates

Deeply informed by Joseph Pilates' principles but embracing a more flexible approach. Familiar classical movements alongside thoughtfully integrated variations using all apparatus — Reformer, Mat, Tower, and Wunda Chair.

Dynamic and adaptable, addressing a wide range of individual needs and fitness goals while promoting core strength, posture, flexibility, and body awareness.
